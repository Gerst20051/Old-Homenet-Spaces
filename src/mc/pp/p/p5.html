TOMORROWS DREAMS

I can feel you now my love
Here in the beat of my heart
Filling me up with your love and warmth.
The sweet nectar thoughts of you
Takes me where I long to be
With you always!

You're only a breath away
Here in the land of our eternal love
Where love knows no boundaries.
Laying my head upon your naked shoulder
I run my hand gently across your chest.
Softly caressing you.

Reaching up to brush your soft
Sensual lips with mine,
Content in this moment within my mind.
I whisper, " Love you."
Looking deep into your baby brown eyes.
I see the dreams of our tomorrows.

Hundreds of them like apples ripening on a tree.
I reach up and carefully pick one, taking a bite.
Savoring the sweet crisp taste.  Longing for the next dream
That finds me in your arms warm and toasty again.
Like awaiting a piece of hot apple pie.

Sweetly scented our love with cinnamon.
For you are the apple of my eye my love.
Planting your seeds within my heart and soul.
I will tend them now my love and watch them grow.
All the dreams of our tomorrows!






MIRACLE

They say it's a miracle what happened to you.
That you won't believe it~but it is all true.
We know where we came from, we knew what to do.
We called on our higher power to flow through you.

When all others gave up we hung on tight.
We encouraged you to continue the fight.
Jesus held you in His loving light.
And became your breathe,your will,your might!

He healed your lungs and breathed life back into you!
God works miracles and this is true.
We kept asking and He gave and gave.
It is true Jesus Christ does save.

You walked within the valley of doom.
It couldn't have been darker in that room.
Then God shone His light down on you.
And in all the glory of His name

He raised you up again and again!!!






MYSTIC MUSE

Riding into the galaxy on mystic white horses.
Galloping in on azure ocean waves.
Halting steed's heels on golden fine sand.
Breath taking view of God's work at hand!

Speak whispers of love to me. Clouds forming above
pictures of all the creatures in love
floating up there on pillows of clouds.
Listen to them their voices grow loud.

Feel the soft breeze brush your face.
And know you are in Heaven's grace.
A transformation of things to yet come
listen for the calling of the sun.

Warmth now envelopes us basking we enjoy.
The hearts fond wishes of the day.
Fine breed of man that sits on mount.
Turning your head and looking about.

Ride with the wind setting you free.
Kick up your heels and run with me.
As we gallop along into our destiny
embracing the Goddess of mystery.






I LOVE YOU DAD

I see you as a man father
In my eyes as a woman.
I feel your strength
Your pride within,
And I love you!

You taught me how to stand up
And fight for what I believe in.
This is so much a part of me today.
My survival within
And I love you for it.

I respect you dad!
For holding us all together as a family.
For providing and believing in us.
For doing your best.
Even when we didn't think it was good enough.

For all the gifts given, to show your love.
For never turning any of us away.
You have always been the rock.
I see you now as the man you've become.
And I thank God for the man you are today!

Father's aren't made to be perfect
They keep learning along the way.
And children aren't made to be perfect.
We keep learning everyday.
Even when we go astray.

So have a happy Father's day dad!
And remember we all love you.
Even though none of us say it enough.
I hold onto the good memories
Of childhood and lessons learnt along the way.

Like when I came back home that day from running
The love you shown to me have always stayed within me.
I never felt closer to you
When you welcomed me home to stay!
I love you Dad!






TWILIGHT'S SWEET YOUTH

Locks of gold dust leave sparkling hair.
Twirling round and going nowhere
Highlight visions fountains of youth
Drink now the serum of my truth.

The idea came as fantasy.
In twilight's zone or mystery?
Reaching out from my entity.
Pleasure seeks the muse released.

Windows doors that didn't open
Key holes seeing into the soul.
Unlocking secrets held within.
Then closing windows once again.

Nobody saw me, nobody wins.
The journey beyond now begins.
Life's riddles we play every day.
Asking questions of what we say.

There are no answers only truths.
Seek it within twilights of youth.
There is no beginning~no end.
No destiny it's only pretend.

Images magic~words that live
Then why can't we learn to forgive?
Freedom's choice a high price to pay.
We watch loved ones die everyday.

We us Christians fighting for right.
Children screaming into the night
Darkness engulfs a world of sin.
No place to go we haven't been.

I a dreamer~makes you one too
Reading my thoughts perhaps the fool.
Life as we live written within
In it together we don't win.

God has a plan for you and me.
Not about life's destiny.
The truth inside you sets you free.
It's not in you it's really me.
Hiding inside~I'll be your friend.
Listening to questions over again.
Finding no answers, which you seek.
There are no answers to what you speak.

Words made alive the way they're wrote.
Painted pictures that gets the vote.
Listen and you will hear the song.
Of your muse now humming along.

Everything old is born again.
Ending starts over ~now begin.
Twirling around going nowhere.
Sparkles of gold locking our hair

Drink from me now~serum of truth
Finding your own twilight's sweet youth!






THE WEEPING WILLOW

I was a thought once.
Put there in your head.
From a magazine you read.
But you didn't give me a second thought.
You said you didn't have a big enough lot.
And now I'm stuck forever on this page.
Weeping willows always.






FROM HER DREAM

Once upon a time in a far away sand castle
Lived a sand princess with golden wings
And pebbles in her toes.
She wondered the beach
Of yesterdays past and sang sweet lullabies
Of her souls flaming desires and longing
Melodies of the hearts hour glass.

As time's passions passed her by.
She dreamt of the wet beaches
And twilight's orange horizons.
Warmed by her heated heart.
She awakes to feel his hands gently
molding her with sand and water.
Forming her lips and nose.


Brushing off the sand left over
Once again from her smashed dream.






COLORING WITH CRAYONS

Somewhere between the crayons
Lye's a little girl dreaming in her room.
Coloring a yellow picture of Donald Duck.
In books of Looney toons,
Quake! Quake! Quake!

Lying on her belly with knees bent up
Swinging in the air.
Smiling barefoot without a care.
Takes me back now in time there.

     Filling the picture's in
With magical visions making me spin.
Memories of days past,
Sleep-overs and playing log on the moist green grass.

Tickles me to think of it at all.
Camping out in the leaky gray old tent.
Giggling and laughing telling spooky stories in black night fall.
Midnight trips to the bathroom stumbling we went.

Screaming and pretending to be afraid, we'd flee
From my brothers who scared us all mercilessly,
Tripping in the dark, careful now don't trip.
Hurry run back to the safety of flickering orange wick.

In my room now once again,

Nighttime talks with my sister back then.
While I sat there and brushed her silky long brown hair
Next morning not remembering a word that was said!
Talking in my sleep instead. With secrets I kept.

Stuffed animals scattered all over, my side of the room.
The long old dresser split down the middle to share.
Hers was neat and mine-scattered papers everywhere.
These are the memories that go through my head.

When I was once young and lived the story told.
Each day a new adventure waiting to unfold.
Oh, how I love to color my world to this day!
In search of the little girl dreaming away.
Lost within the colors of rainbows past.
Holding onto memories that will always last.






YOUR SEARCH WITHIIN

I watch you as you struggle to hold onto your pride.
Fighting within but walking with that same stride.
A man fighting to live and prolong the life you know.
Feeling such turmoil but cant let it show.

Confused and frustrated angers slave.
I see your sadness and yet you remain brave.
You appear to be empty within your soul.
Yet have a place to go.

I try to get close and comfort you.
But you push me away with the things you do.
Remains the cold rock that you've always been.
Needing no one not even a friend.

I pray you will feel the comfort inside your soul.
That God has put there, to feel his warm glow.
Where you had been you will never know.
Just believe me, when I say you are a miracle.

I never told you how proud I am of you.
For fighting the odds and making it through.
You battled satan and saw the light.
I knew then that things would come out right.

Look deep within inside of you.
Your Holy comforter will guide you.
I wish I could give you the answers you seek.
But I am only another lost sheep.

Trust in the God we all prayed to.
He is the one that pulled you through.
Reach out your hand and trust in him.
You are alive today and walk with him.

We are all his children in our faith.
Continue to reach for the lighted gate
Ask and you shall receive.
The hardest part is you must believe.
Your search within will lead you to him.
Just ask and receive and let him in!






  THANK YOU JESUS

I am not worthy of Your love
It is stronger than anything I know of.
I fail time and time again.
And yet You forgive me of my sin.

You don't ask much of me in return.
But you know my heart and the way it yearns.
My faith was built on the rock you built.
Open and loving not up on a shelf.

I proclaim my love of you through poetry.
And I send your messages out that come to me.
It is not for me to make any believe.
This in life they must achieve.

I only know when I truly call upon you.
You have always answered and the message got through.
This is my reward for my strong faith.
With You I believe and know I am safe.

I know You by the feeling inside of me.
The warmth and love You give onto me.
I trust in You I already know.
I no longer question, I ask and go!

I keep my mind open to listen to You.
Which I know others also will try too.
I believe in Your name and Your Holy light.
And in miracles You give of life.

Your love I can hardly explain.
It's too awesome to contain.
Thank You Jesus is all I can proclaim.
Nothing now will ever be the same.






CAN YOU KEEP A SECRET

Can you keep a secret?
And promise not to tell?
Can you keep it all to yourself?
You're the only one I've told.
But you'll have to keep it as you grow old!

Yes, I can keep a secret,
I won't tell a soul.
I'll keep it a secret until we both grow old.
I only told my sister and she promised not to tell.
So she will keep the secret just as well.

I whispered it in her ear.
And I'm sure no one could hear.
Except her best friend Chris
Who was standing there.
But don't you worry any.

She promised not to tell
She'd keep your secret just as well.
I only let it slip just once the other day.
I was so excited I forgot what I was going to say.
When talking to my mother I told her not to tell.

So don't you worry all is really well.
But next time I wouldn't tell me
If I was you! 
I never could keep a secret!
Could you?


 



MR. SAND MAN

Please help me sleep. 
I'm tired of counting the fuzzy white sheep.
Take me to your sand dooms where magical castles are formed.
Take me to the tides rising in the orange morn.

Count with me now until I drift away.
In an hourglass of time slowly drained.
Sprinkle sand dust on my feet and watch me play.
A game of tag like when I was young!

You're it! Run man run!
Ouch, I'm barefoot in the heat of the sun.
You can't catch me.
I won! I won!

Tell me about sand castles once built.
Swept away with one giant wave!
And the pretty seashells collected on those days.
(Yawning), what did you say?

I'm feeling so groggy now, one, two, and three.
Please stay a while longer
While we build sand castles in my sleep.
SNORE! SNORE!






THROUGH THESE CHILD'S EYES

Small hands clasp
as we gaze upon the fairy pony
white wings spread wide
with huge white feathers
ready to take flight.

Special these moments
sharing the same dream
as if we were one heart
one soul.
One being.
~
Burning this image
this memory of delight
into our very soul
the child within knows
the truths in fairy tales.
~
Others will grow old
as days pass them by
while visions dance in our heads
of flights of the fairy steeds.
Seen only through these child's eyes.






  "LOVE'S SALVATION"

I write not of the thunder that roars in my heart
Or the drenching rain
But the sprinkle of hope
That falls from the heavens.


The rainbow that is so close now
I can almost grasp its salvation.
The promise of everlasting!
I seek not the pot of gold.


But the hereafter the happily ever-
The song I hear within your heart
And the beating of your soul with the rain!
Freedom lies within the soft, cool, breeze


That steadily carries your words
Like lifesavers to my lips
I am walking along the beach
Barefooted in the wet sand


Picking up seashells and pretty rocks.
Passing by your massive sand castles
Making believe I am there on
Your island of love!


Can you hear it, the beating of the rain?
I can hear it calling your name.
A sprinkle of hope, falling from the heavens,
Love's salvation found in the rainbow's
Promise of love's salvation!

I weave of lovers sweet embrace
A gentle hand upon my face
A loving gaze that warms my soul

I weave arrows of cupid's love

With golden rods and silver lace
It finds your heart and penetrates!


A woven heart now beats as one
For now our journey has begun!!
It's magic wrapped all in kisses
And fairy godmother wishes.

"PASSING ON THE STREET"

She's just a person of the street
With no home and nothing to eat.
Sitting there against the alley wall,
I stop to wonder-what caused her fall.

She looked some how displaced,
Adorned in purple velvet-out of place.
On this hot august day
I stop to look away.

There I was staring again
At her purple velvet headband
Holding together her matted ebony hair.
Please forgive me as I stare.

Such a youthful face-
Staring off into space.
Looking around her at all
As a look of a child in awe!

She has no home-no family.
Where are the roots to her tree?
She travels along with the wind
Blowing in my direction again.

Like a floating autumn leaf,
Reaching out from beneath
A bare tree standing alone
She has no place to call her own.

I was like all the rest that day.
Hurrying along my way!
Just a stranger on passing on the street!
And now she haunts my sleep.

She had no home-no place to sleep.
I offered nothing-not a meal to eat.
Now I'm empty-hungry-incomplete.
Ashamed to be a stranger-
Passing on the street.






 "HER WILLOW EYES"

Will you read this poem of willow eyes?
And see her anguish in disguise
Hidden under her veil of tomorrows
And promises that are borrowed!

Do you see her graceful simplicity?
Sitting under the weeping tree.
With all her thoughts drifting free
Holding on to her dignity.

See her swaying so miserably?
This vision of beauty a willow tree
Her willow eyes setting free
Her pain and anguish willowing!






 "SOUL SEARCHER"

You search for poets of the soul
You think you've found them but you don't know
What lies deep beneath their skin
The souls that won't let you in!

You think you know how poets feel
When you read their words
You think they're real
They create emotions that you can't still.

But hidden beneath the rhyming scheme
And pretty words that make you dream
Is where you find the soulful being!
Behind the painted dressing screen.

They make you happy, joyful and sad
They may be someone's mom or dad
They may be a child left alone again
They may be childless and hurt within.

You think you find them on the page
But they are hidden.
Clothed in invisible ink behind the scene
They will make you stop and even dream.

So next time you read a poem
And you think you see, I beseech you to look within
Behind the painted dressing screen
Is where you'll find the soulful being!

Sometimes they hurt and sometimes they're sad.
You may not like what you may see.
But it's all a part of one's reality.
Poets of the soul both breathe and hurt.

They laugh and cry and even flirt.
They fall in love and break up too.
They dream and build mountains just like you!
So when in search of poets of the soul
Remember it may be someone you already know!!






I AM

Your message is so very simple
read between the lines of sin
that chases hearts of aloneness with desire
but flesh is only flesh upon this earth.

Ours the kingdom of wisdom inside.
Matters not what road is taken there.
What matters is your arrival,
with open hands and hearts truth!

Yeah, you think yee are the only one spoken to.
Then you are the fool
for he lives within us all
that listen and believe.

Passion is not the sin we think it to be
for it is that passion we worship Him
the one almighty
that within each breathe we take.

Run not then from your passions fairies.
They will carry you in your dreams
and bring you to the place of belief
where you can worship all in my name.

Judge not that which you question so.
You will know when the time has come,
that you are indeed a chosen one.
By your faith you will stand beside him.

There is the truth then if you have eyes to see,
and ears to listen to your own soul.
Reach beyond what which you Penn
and behold I am!






GOD'S ANSWER

Aye yee know the play of minds games
upon the lassies fair
that which draws you in
and transforms the conscious mind

yet tis your heart that yearns
not the lassies you saught
for you are in search of something
most of them are not.

Your passion runs deep
the eternal flame within
burning torch that it is
flames hearts souls again.

I am God of all things
and answer all who fall upon their knees
calling up on Me
I am the spirit of messages you release.

Even upon the merry hill.
And yee are blessed to have called this day
for others heard the message if you did not
trust in My words that you so easily penn.

I am the passion that drives you within.
Your imprisoned body I claim as mine.
My child, by your faith
Judge not yourself or others.

Your gentle heart does not go unnoticed.
You give freely to those you love
Tormented to see their agony
that which you cannot escape

Free yourself within
release your soul to forgiveness
stand tall and lean on My foundation
you know the way have you forgot?

You will know Me by My love
that shines into the night
glowing within a world of darkness
that few can see the light.

Go now and meditate and I will come unto you.
Did I not hear your cry in the night?
My child you know Me and mine.
Stay in the light of faith.

There you shall find me within you!





HIS MUSIC

Haunts me as I read these words penned
over and over again.
Listening to his music my soul cries out in unity.
Feeling the depth of his reality.
Grasping to be held within his embrace this night.
I hear his voice sweet echoing onto me. 
Laying here beside him feeling his breath now upon my cheek
I turn my head towards him with puckered lips.
Tracing them with my tongue savoring the softness there
while his scent fills my room with
a masculine smell of chaps aftershave and desire.
I reach out to him with a smile content knowing the feeling
of serenity when held within his warm embrace.
  And I cry out loud for the sweet agony.
Abruptly I awake and squint my eyes to see.
Looking around me in the darkness to find once again I am alone
and him a ghost within my mind.
  How does the heart bear the aloneness?
I put my arms around myself and give myself a hug.
Pulling the covers up around me
then curl up into a ball.
Tears now escaping from my eyes.
I tell myself I am not alone
and here I cry myself to sleep
to his music I heard once so bitter sweet.





 
I BELIEVE IN ANGELS

I believe in angels.
God sends them with love.
I called a hundred of them in one night.
They remained at Bob's side
They protected him
With their heavenly wings.
During his terrible experience.
God gives us special gifts.
He sends us his heavenly light.

I believe in Angels. I believe in God.
I believe. I believe, I believe
In their love.

THE LADY

She is a lady wearing many faces.
Clothed in a lacy black sleeveless dress that clings to her form.
Red sequin purse strapped over her shoulder
Brown bobby cut hair curled to her face.
Wearing that deep velvet flowered purple hat.
That father gave her.
Her head tilted just slightly.
Long dangly earrings hanging down
Gives her a look of prestige.
And wealth.

She wears vibrant beads,
Of a colorful bib neck lass hanging around her neck
Swooping down upon her breast.
How many forms have these clung to?
Only she knows the travels she's seen.
Only she knows the broken hearts torn.
The unkempt engagements along the way
The promises broken
Contained upon her still form
So full of life lived.

As well as those of laughter shared and promises kept.
Where has she been in her worldly travels?
How many dressing rooms has she adorned?
Many eyes have turned to glance
Upon her still life like form.
So perfect in heart outward appearance
Pleasant upon the viewer's eyes
Makes one wonder about the life lived.
Preserved upon this lady
Pieces of someone's past
Now she sits quietly in an antique store
With so much more life
Yet to be lived
This lady's given heritages
Preserves the past a while longer
As she awaits her next journey.
And to think she was once just a manikin in the Good Will Store.
