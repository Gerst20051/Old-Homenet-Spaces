STAND TALL AND SPEAK YEE NOW MY NAME


Stand on my serene sea-green mountain of faith
Speaking your mind to all- in me be ye safe.
To tell the world of -my love, my warmth, my grace
Blessed is Kingdom's gate- Heaven-my grace-full place.

Follow me, we pray believe now in my name.
I profess my love to all now as-I proclaim!
Let it said to the entire world-I still reign.
I am God of love's pureness, not devils pain.

Stand tall and speak ye now my name!
Listen not to anyone's lies or false beliefs.
Open your hearts and minds and spirits to believe.
I am your true God, loving and forgiving.
All that you ask in my name I give thee.

Watching now glide in on pure white shooting stars
Angels descending down on earth with just cause
Bringing many blessings, my gifts and-my laws.
None live in this world without flesh sins and flaws.

Stand tall and speak ye now my name!
Follow me we pray believe now in my name.
I profess my love to all now as I proclaim!
Let it said to the entire world-I still reign.
I am God, of love's pureness not devils pain.

 





REFLECTIONS OF GOD

The stars glitter like diamonds in the sky.
Rich and too expensive to really buy
Made and adorned on each person's face.
Leaving smiles that can't be erased.

The floating clouds form pictures of awesome things.
Like angels flying with heavenly silver wings.
And pictures of Jesus riding up in the clouds
Coming on a white horse and chariot galloping down.

I see many formations way up high.
The infinity of the vast beautiful indigo sky
I watch as the sparrows of life fly by
And I stop to ponder why?

Has God given us such a gift?
That all we need to do is lift
Up our moist eyes and pray into Heaven's
Window and see into the souls of man?

For we are all made in likeness of him.
Therefore he is like a chameleon.
He loves both the son and daughter.
Like loving Fathers au~too.

The pureness is in the hand of God's love.
Shown in all that is made above.
All the living and breathing things
Holy mirrors reflecting
GOD!






 
BLANK PAGES

I leave all thoughts behind
Pages within my mind
No direction or time.

Dead all the metaphors
Images once before
Left feeling like a whore

my muse please rescue me.
Your pages breaking free
have blown telepathically

anything to consume.
But they are empty too.
No trace of the ink once you!

Whom do I try to fake?
There is no real escape.
Cold now my body aches.

Darkness closes on me
Pitch black the full moon beams.
Never ending doom seems

Blinded no sense where I've been.
My once empty thoughts penned!
Thoughts, tattered by the wind.

I now the blank pages
Once again!







"FINGER PAINTED LEAVES"

Maples stand by fully clothed
With their vibrant finger painted leaves
That covers their chilled trunk and limbs
As we watch them slowly floating
Through the air alighting
Upon the green ground below!
Raked leaf piles stand ready
For children to run and jump into them
Disappearing into their soft feather like cushion.
Magnificent in their bounty of colors like a
Rainbow spreading joy to all
That looks upon them as their clothed limbs slowly,
Disrobe leaving them barren for
Winter's dormant stage!







"THE FALL OF WINTER"

In the fall of winter
I watch upon the diminishing
Colors
Of nature's cycles of life
As budding blooms slowly fade
Leaving a brown barren look,
Made spooky for
Halloween
In it's dormant stage.
I feel the cool breeze brush against me
As I gaze across the country side
Of cleared corn fields from summer's harvest.
Anticipating
The chill of winter to come,
I find closure here in the painted falling leafs
Bringing an end to summer's delights
And the warmth of
Sunshine kisses.
The beginning of winter's purity,
Brings a sense of calmness to me
As I reminisce on summers accomplishments and
The joy of another season with it's changing crayon colors
Carpeting the ground as nature prepares her-self for
The fall of winter!

BABY STEPS

We take things for granted when we are young.
What seems so natural when life begun.
Like moving around and learning to crawl.
Standing up and taking that first fall.
It's like that for others when the body lays still.
It forgets how to move or the things that are real.
Your mind is a power that controls it all.
Your will is the most powerful of all.
You lay there and struggle and they all see you weep.
But I stand and smile you moved your feet.
I tell them they don't know you the way that we do.
That you can do anything you have a mind too.
Baby steps~Baby steps happening everyday.
I stop and thank Jesus and pray and pray.
A miracle of God of love and giving.
A new spirit in you that is living.
Your spirit is strong and we know you will fight.
And I keep telling you everything is going to be allright.
We're taking you home we don't care what they say.
Baby steps~Baby steps all the way.
Baby steps home~Home to stay!

JOURNEY OF LOVE

Come my love touch my heart
With your eyes that reach within my soul!
Feel the flow of love between us
Cascading down on a shimmering waterfall
Of our emotions spilling into a pool
Of hot springs!

As we tread through the warm
Contentment there of one another
While looking upon the white lilies
Floating on the surface
A clear reflection of our love!
Listening to the sounds of nature's music
Calling out to us while the birds chatter
And play a fluted lover's serenade.

Basking in the sunshine
Our bodies entwined within a sweet embrace
Savoring the taste of honeysuckles
Still on our lips
While inhaling each other's sweet scent!
Taking us to a place in time
Only we can share as we ride
The warmth of our emotions into
The hot springs of our love's journey.

WITHIN THE WAIF'S CALLING

She searches for her soul's inspiration
For hearts enlightenment
Feeling lost for words again
She summons up the poets sprites.
Tumbling in hopping over one and other.
All little friends playing leapfrog with no bothers.
They test her mind for rhyme~they test it for the reasons
Making her choose to be wise
Or have some fun and go disguised 
As the sprites of poetic form
Winning the elfin's prize for mystic playgrounds
Psychedelic colors twirling around
Little miniature toy dolls and carnival rides
With enchantment and games.
Elfin's everywhere running around going insane.
Giggling, laughing holding hands.
Twirling around and letting go~
There you are where ever you land.
Fun to go to this place of play land.
Listening to the waif within
Playing the elfin's flute once again.
The melody drawing you to dance within
Sucking you into the components of sound.
Rhyme and meter~flow and roll~ing along.
The main ingredients of the poetic form
You can hear their music in the words they speak.
Soft and flowing beauty~oh so deep.
They tip toe in on the fluted waves.
Making you feel all warm and dazed.
Stealing your dreams and hopes.
Displaying them on the pages for folk.
Pixies enlightening your soul~telling words where to go
On a Merry Go Round until they are all found.
Disappearing into the land of enchantment dreams.
Within the hearts soul of fantasy~
Good-bye my sweet little pixies~
Good-bye my sweet el-fin's of poetry.
Sweet music plays within the waif's calling.







WHO AM I?

"Who are you", he said to me.  'Who are you trying to be?"
I said,"me!" I just want to be me."
But he did reply. " I like you for who you are."
I said, "I know and that's just fine,
But others have their doubt.
They do not see me inside looking out.
I just want to be me."
"I don't understand who it is you want to be?
Please explain it to me then."
"Me, I said, " I just want to be me.
Why won't they just let me be me?"
They think you can cope with life that way.
But that is a slow way of dyeing.
And you loose whom you really are inside.
I just want to be me.  It's so simple don't you see?"
"I'm not asking much really.
I just want to be who I am and free.
Free to look pretty if I want or go on a treasure hunt.
Free to open and close the doors without them shutting me out.
I want to be able to pick up the phone
And call you just because
And not have to explain all the people that I love.
To hold my head high and not stick it in a hole
Like gophers do.
To make plans and dreams and wishes come true.
I want to be in control don't you see?
I just want to be me.
I don't want to control you only me!
There's a difference don't you see?
They think they know me. But they don't see who I am.
They all judge me and condemn.
Hell I don't even have but one friend."
I just want to be me.  Sometimes I wonder
What it would be like. Who then would I be?
Would I be very different if I was really me?
Perhaps that isn't reality. 
Or maybe a mirage I conjured up in me.
But I am thirsty to be all that I am.
And all that I am to become
But I can't be the who you think I am anymore.
I have to be me. Don't you see?"
It's killing me softly not knowing how
And if I can be who I am.
And in the end he said to me.
"I still don't understand who you want to be?"

" But don't change who you are. 
You have a caring and warm heart
And you are a good person and friend.
Don't let them all suck you in."
And I thought that is who I am!
It's all I ever wanted to be.  Me!

I WILL WIN

Imagine in your mind
We live in another time.
You sit atop a pure white stallion
Holding your head high

Your hair auburn red
Tossed from the wind.
Wearing that boyish grin
Smiling over at me again.

I give you a wink or two
Just to let you know I see you.
Excited now with the anticipation
Of our first kiss.

The race is about to begin.
Man and horse through tough terrain.
The horses are ready and stomping their feet.
Bobbing their heads up and down.

You smile at me and say "I will win!"
The gun sounds and the race begins.
I watch you galloping away on the air.
Knowing my destiny is in your hands now.

I hold my breath as the horses are headed home.
Looking and searching to see the great white
There in the distance I see the earth moving
And you lean into the head taking the lead.

Cheers sounding all around now.
You there with a single white rose and smiling
Reaching out you lift me up onto your lap
And hand me the white rose.

Leaning forward our lips gently brush
And an inner rush comes over us.
I look dreamily into your eyes.
And softly say to you, "I will win!"

 "AMIDST A PUDDLE"

I am but a rush of water falling
Aimlessly upon the ground!
A mist, a puddle that never seems to be found!
I am pouring down with emotions
That slowly forms into a light rainfall.
And yet I am forever remaining in the puddle
Of sorrow taking on more of my tomorrows!
There is a cloud of yesterday hanging over my head
And lightening waiting to strike me dead!
I am treading water alone amidst an inner whirlpool that
Threatens to suck me down into its endless drain.
I am shapeless to my passers by that step within me
In a hurry to continue on their way
I am but a teardrop that has fallen down
Falling, falling gently to the ground.
Amidst a puddle!

THE LIGHT WITHIN

They say they know me but they don't care.
They are shallow and going nowhere.
 I strive for life in the experiences I share.
Living in an asylum where nobody cares.
 I am illusive and sometimes go unseen. 
Yet I am but a screen of your imagination.
Some of you know the way through the mesh.
But many of others have only regrets.
 This is so sad to think that you know the secrets of life.
In truth you are in the darkness of night.
You will look for the light and won't see it there.
 For you already think you are there. 
Beware the trouble that your darkness brings.
 It is what controls you on earth, the human beings.
.  The light is Holy and reaches out to you.
