THE MAN WITHOUT A DREAM


Shallow and cold as a stream a man without a dream
Sitting upon a pedestal better than the rest of all us dream weavers.
We plant the dreams of our tomorrows
And get chipped nails and soiled hands from our labors.
He stands on rich black dirt doesn't have to change his shirt.
Put his brain in place of his brawn and lived his whole life knowing
Right from wrong!
He saw the black and white never saw the gray in things.
Never had to give up or fight.
Worked hard but forgot how to play.
Bought a house and raised a family.
This man without a dream could only see yesterday
He couldn't see tomorrow.  This man of power
Always wanting to control. He knew what it was like to possess
The material things but his life was empty.
He had no dreams
Shallow and cold like the stream running through him.
No compassion in his soul.
Where will you go when your stream runs dry?
Who will then wipe the tears from your eyes?
When will you search deep within your soul?
Tell me now where do the years go?
I see you as a lonely man with all your dignity.
Sometimes I see you reaching out to me.
But you are distant from me now.
And I have more fields to plow.
Perhaps you'll plant a seed or two.
And I'll even watch them grow for you.
But your heart has grown shallow as the stream runs through it.
And you untouchable now there on your pedestal
I bow to you with dignity.
But I wouldn't trade all the dreams in the world
No not give up a single one.
For fear my heart too will dry up.
And I might become just like you
The woman without dreams!







THESE SHORE LESS WORDS


I hear only your silence on this dark lonely night.
It floods me with frigid water
As I sit here to write.
Paddling my way through the waves of your rejection
That cuts deep into my bleeding heart.
I struggle to stay afloat upon these shore less words.
And listen in hopes to hear you calling out to me.
But I wait in vain for I hear no answer from thee.
And this silence now sounds like the beating of your heart.
Traveling alone through this tunnel of night's blackness.
No warmth from you to guide my way
No place to turn.  No place to stay.
I keep afloat upon my inner tube of hope.
But your only answer is these cold silent words.
I cant stand the silence nothing can be worse.
Just a soft whisper a word or two.
Any message as long as it's from you.
You're killing me softly
As I struggle to stay afloat upon
These shore less words.







NEVER LET IT DIE


My sweet, sweet love
Where has these years gone by?
How many tears have you dried?
Come my love and wade into the depth of my soul.
Quench your weary heart and become whole
Once again
Snuggle into my body and soul
Warm your hands with the heat of our love.
Fear not for I will be the lighthouse unto your soul.
I will show you the way into eternity of our emotions.
There you will experience our greatest desires.
Clasp my hand my love.
Wrap your body round me.
Let me guide you to me this night.
Hold fast to your dreams of us.
For a moment can change a life of anguish.
I believe in you and I.
And will never let our love die.






OUR ETERNAL BEATING HEART

Come my love clasp my hand within yours.
Walk beside me now and guide our souls along the cobbled path of time.
Lead us through the birthing of our dreams and desires.
Once fantasies played out in our mind
Like watching an old romantic movie with a sad ending!
In one brief moment of ecstasy
The heat of your breath catches mine as I brush my lips gently upon yours.
I feel the warmth of your body as I press closer to you.
Heated with the passion of our desires.
Listen do you hear the wind echoing our love from the heavens above?
Down into a deep canyon of forever.
Draw me closer now in your warm embrace.
Shelter me from the cold reality of our separate worlds.
Enter now for all of eternity in the joining of our souls as one.
Listen to our hearts beat as one pa pum, pa pum and know it is our destiny.
This journey through time now together locked within each other's entity.
Through past and present life's and into the next our love shall endure.
I will be there my love; search and you shall find me beside you always
Within our souls eternal beating heart!!!





MY GIFT TO YOU FOR ALL OF TIME

What can I give thee sister mine?  At this very Holy time!
What gift of joy or pure delight, to touch your soul this Christmas night?
I confess I've searched the stores both new and old.
But have found no treasures to behold.
So I give thee sister from my heart,
A written verse, a word or two
A part of my soul to carry always with you!
Wrapped in many colors and when opened,
Burst into a rainbow of stars that float around within you.
Bringing with it God's peace and love!
I give thee friendship sister mine
To last forever into time!
And I pledge thee the best gift of all this day!
I give my love and bond of sister hood.
No matter what may come or go.
Through life's journey that takes it's tow.
I shall remain as I've always been.
Your loving sister and life long friend!!!
I thank you for being there for me.
For listening when I need you to.
For spending time with me like you do.
For letting your guard down and letting me in.
And for being my sister my life long friend!!!

I love you!!!!






TOMORROWS DREAMS

I can feel you now my love
Here in the beat of my heart
Filling me up with your love and warmth.
The sweet nectar brings thoughts of you
That takes me to you, where I long to be
With you always!

You're only a breath away
Here in the land of our eternal love
Where love knows no boundaries.
Laying my head upon your naked shoulder
I run my hand gently across your chest.
Softly caressing you.

Reaching up to brush your soft
Sensual lips with mine,
Content in this moment within my mind.
I whisper, `` Love you."
Looking deep into your baby brown eyes.
I see the dreams of our tomorrows.

Hundreds of them like apples ripening on a tree.
I reach up and carefully pick one, taking a bite.
Savoring the sweet crisp taste.  Longing for the next dream
That finds me in your arms warm and toasted again.
Like awaiting a piece of hot apple pie.

Sweetly scented our love with cinnamon.
For you're the apple of my eye my love.
Planting your seeds within my heart and soul.
I will tend them now my love and watch them grow.

All the dreams of our tomorrows!

I AM A BIT ECCENTRIC

Perhaps I am, a bit eccentric with the things that I do.
Compulsively collecting costume jewelry.
With my vintage jewelry sparkling everywhere like diamonds.
But the joy it brings to me, no pleasure can compare.
Fake jewels of colored rhinestones and gemstones to.
Glittering where they are displayed
On an old K-Mart rotating watch case,
That I picked up for fifty dollars. My husband says I collect junk.
But it's for the beholder to see. Each unique piece is a jewel to me.
Perhaps I should have been born in another place and time.
There's a Victorian lady living inside of me.
Adorning her jewels displaying them proudly.
Each piece when found is part of the journey.
Each one has it's own history. A life existed that owned them before.
Who knows what secrets these jewels store!
Muli colors of the rainbow, yes I own them all.
Some blue, some pink, some green, some white and shimmering.
Era descent, black and purple containing stones of mystery,
It's the thrill of the hunt, the journey that makes it all worth it to me.
Yes, perhaps I am a bit eccentric. That's for you to decide.
But I'll go on collecting and display them with pride.





MY BIRTHDAY GIFT


I awoke with the normal morning routine of sloppy dog kisses and the sun shining
Through my window, then there came such a clatter,
I had to jump up to see what the heck was the matter. There as bold as
Could be was a fat, brown squirrel staring at me, eating the bird feed I put out.
Was he ever a sight for my sore eyes!
Cause I hadn't seen him in one long cold winters month!
I had given up hope and thought him dead along the roadside.
How I had missed watching him sit boldly and eat corn and sunflower seeds.
With his tiny feet and hands.
While my dogs would bark and jump up and down excitedly at my French door
where they could see out. That's how I knew it was my squirrel that returned to
me.
He wasn't bothered by those dogs no way -no how -no soiree!
And I thought what a wonderful birthday gift my squirrel visiting me.
And letting me know he is okay after all. Knowing this was the most precious gift
of all.
To know he is alive and well. It has put a warm glow upon my face.
And a song in my heart and a lesson learnt to never give up hope.
Nature has a way of taking care of it's own.
I can only await his return when once again he puts a smile on my face
And brightens up my world with his squirrelly self.







LISTEN TO THE CHILDREN CRY

Listen to the children crying in the darkness of this night.
Another lost child in a woods full of tall dark oak trees. Asking for their identity.
Asking does anybody love me? Does anybody care?
For crying out loud is there anyone out there!
How can you give to one child and not all the rest?
How can you claim to be a good family man and leave those behind?
You know the one's hiding in the shadows created in another time.
The one's you all deny and say but they aren't mine!
Who's then are they? To whom do they belong?
Being raised with only a mom. Ignored by a whole family.
Not knowing their family tree. Longing to know from where they come.
Not asking much really in return. Just an open door to walk within
A place at the table to sit and talk, a man to call father and ask about their day
What kind of man are you to turn away? What are you so afraid of anyways?
Afraid to face your own past I see. Afraid to look into the mirror and see who is
staring
Back at you! Afraid to do what is right aren't you? It is your reality that you fear.
You cannot change the past! But the future you can steer.
Don't look the other way like you've done before. You will be the one to answer
in the end. Would you risk it all and just turn away again? Or will you be who
you are, a Father this day!!
To that little lost child that was forgotten along the way?
Can you hear her cry in the middle of the night? Do you toss and turn and yet
have no regret? I can hear her sweet crying in the breeze this night.
She's calling out to you and praying you'll make things right.
Oh, how can you stand to hear her crying in the breeze, it's such a sad picture,
sobbing
On her knees, for God's sake doesn't anyone else hear her but me? Will
someone please
Set this childfree? Give her back her identity!! Be a father to all your kids!!
Don't pick and choose because of your life or wife!
Be a man and take some responsibility! Love this child unconditionally!!
And for God's sake do it for you as much as for her.
Touch your soul and humanity. Don't let this child go for another day!
Take the time to show you care. Say you love them and then be there!!
So sad to listen to the children cry in the night so many have already given up their
Fight.
It's just not right.
All these children crying in the night!






WHERE IS THE LAUGHTER

Where is the laughter?
I saw in your eyes
Now clouded with responsibilities
There's rain in the for-cast for you and me.

Do you remember yesterday?
When we were young and both foolish.
How I loved your smile back then.
It warmed me like a toasty campfire.

But where is the laughter now my love?
Where does the laughter go to hide?
Your smile turned now to a frown.
Eyes cast down.

No sparkle in your eyes only fresh dew.
I see rain clouds coming from you.
It's so lonely here baby in this land of pouring rain.
And I'm so afraid I'll go insane.

Just a smile from you is all I really need.
A sign of the coming rainbow, please take heed.
Journey back with me now in time
Remember when you were mine?

And the presence of each other made us smile with content
We never knew where the time ever went.
But we had time back then to enjoy the simple things.
Like swinging on the park swings.

Walking hand in hand. A woman and her man!
Funny how I never realized back then
Your smile would be worth its weight in gold!
As the years pass us by and we grow old!

REALITY ON A WEDNESDAY MORNING

Where are you muse?  You are illusive,
How can I write upon this page without you?
How can I feel what I write and write what I feel?
There is no creativity alive within. You must be in your den.
I keep trying to conjure you up. But I'm about beaten with these words
That conveys nothing to anyone in this vast space in time.
Perhaps your locked up for your crime of stealing my creativity
Kidnapped and held prisoner within my own emotionless sphere
feeling nothing just being here.
You are like a child-playing hide and seek.
But when I call all in free you run from me.
Perhaps your playing teeter-totter with the child within
And don't hear me calling out to you.
Or perhaps you lost your voice and have laryngitis.
And cant convey what's on your mind.
Or worse yet perhaps you've entered into another time.
What ever the case may be please take pity on me.
I have words to write this day.  And emotions to convey
They're building up inside of me.
I've lost the call All Free,

Reality on a Wednesday Morning…

Holding On For Yesterday

I will miss you my love
I know you will someday be above
Watching over me on a pure white puffy cloud.
Sitting there and still looking proud.

I don't know what I'll do.
To go on in this world without you
You're always near, close to my heart.
I don't want us to ever have to part.

I'll miss the way we love to play fight.
And how I know things will turn out right.
Making up yesterday's mistakes
Kissing me good morning before it's too late.

While you hold on for another yesterday.
I will continue to bow and pray.
The little things matter the most now.
And I know we'll make it through this somehow.

Funny how when I see flowers I think of you
Watering the pots the way you do.
The scent of them always reminds me of you.
Our yellow roses sweet scent of perfume

The little things mean so much.
That playful look and tender touch
Accidentally spraying me with the garden hose.
The way we feel about each other nobody knows.

The peaceful man I see inside of you.
The one I've grown accustomed too.
I just want you to know I've always loved you.
And I don't know what I'll ever do.

You see I'm holding on to another yesterday.
And I'm praying you'll keep holding onto today.
For when life on this earth is through
I'll still go on loving you!
