Organization Codes
Back

These suffixes at the end of an e-mail address or web site show what kind of organization or institute is at the address or site.

Code Meaning
ac  academic organization
com commercial organization
edu educational institute
gov government body
int international organization
mil military organization
net organizations involved in the operations of the internet
org nonprofit organization
