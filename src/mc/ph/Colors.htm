Colors

Below you'll find the name and hex values for all colors. Use these values for your font and background color tags when coding your page. A few colors can be referred to using names rather than hex values. A typical color-definition in HTML would look like this:

<body bgColor="#FF8C67">

The # simply states that the following numbers are hexadecimal. The first two digits are the amount of red. Digits 3 and 4 specify the amount of the color green, digits 5 and 6 specify the amount of blue.

Keep in mind that only 216 colors are "browser-safe". "Browsersafe" colors are colors that looks the same on every type of browser. Different browsers run on different platforms and interpret colors differently. "Browsersafe" colors are the colors made out of pairs of 00, 33, 66, 99, CC and FF.
