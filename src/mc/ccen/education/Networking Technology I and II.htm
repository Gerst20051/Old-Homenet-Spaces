Networking Technology I and II

Course Description:

Students will be provided a broad base of networking technology skills. Emphasis will be placed on skills needed to design and maintain networks, install cabling, and configure routers and switches. Students will receive hands on training setting up, installing, and troubleshooting Nortel networks.

Course Description:

Network Engineering Technology II introduces the fundamentals of wide area networks, advanced muter configurations, switched networks, VLANS, and simple network management protocol. Students will receive hands-on experience in all of these areas. Presentations and communication skills needed be a network engineer will also be emphasized. Appropriate work-based strategies include job shadowing, internship, and apprenticeship. Hands on experiences and VICA/TSA leadership activities provide opportunities to enhance classroom instruction.
