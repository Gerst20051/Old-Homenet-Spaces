MASHED EMOTIONS

Peeling each emotion carefully.
Slicing and dicing them up one by one.
Whipped up to a creamy texture.
No sorting through them now.
All together lumped into a bowl.
Warm mashed emotions here.
All smashed together like hot mashed potatoes.
Whipped up with milk and butter.
Still warm from the stove
Not sure what to serve with it.
Lightly salted to add flavor.
The taste lasting on my tongue
NOTHING LIKE HOMEMADE MASHED EMOTIONS
Skip the gravy this doesn't need any.
Already gooie enough on its own
Waiting to be devoured in a meal.
Completely gobbled up.
Warm mashed emotions
Food for the soul…







REMEMBER ME

Hello my love it's me again
Seems I get lost within the time
Of our different worlds
But this time machine I'm on has taken me
Back into our world once again
A time for love and dreams
You draw me here my love.
To holding hands and smiling faces
A place of campfires and quiet moonlit walks
I can see the orange flames dancing in the night breeze.
As if playing with each other
As I snuggle closer to the warmth of your body.
Is this then our reality my love?
Or is it just mine within my mind?
Will you walk along the cobbled path of time with me?
Your hand in mine now swinging to and fro
Will you be the one to dry my tears?
In the middle of the night
Will you become a part of my soul?
A part of my destiny
My sweet, sweet love
I have awaited you for so long.
Longing to feel your arms wrapped around me.
Yearning inside to feel complete.
Did you not know how much
Love my heart holds for you?
I have been a way for too long.
Perhaps you have forgotten
The way my lips feel when they brush up against yours.
The tenderness of my nibbling on your lower lip
The feel of our tongues darting into each other's mouth
How sensual your tongue feels as it circles mine.
My heart beating rapidly with desire for your touch
Perhaps you have forgotten the way my warm body feels
Pressed softly up against yours.
The feel of my entity climbing into your body
The emotions that stir our very souls
The raw hunger that takes control
Perhaps you have forgotten
That our bodies ignite with just the slightest touch.
Sending shivers of desire from our heads to our toes.
Can you hear the melody that plays in each of our hearts?
Will you rock to that rhythm with me now baby?
Gently back and forth through the night
Remember my love remember me.







LET ME TELL YOU A SECRET

Let me tell you a secret
That I've always known.
When I die and go to heaven
I won't be alone.
You'll be there beside me
At the pearly white gate
We will know only love
There won't be any hate.
Our souls will join as one
When we enter for all time.
Silken lace made from heaven
We will be eternally entwined.
Your heart will be beating within mine
We will know love's pleasure divine.
And discover the fountain of our youth.
For it is held deep inside. Its name is truth.
The light of the world is the love that we share.
Everyone that love's will get there.
Every soul will beat as one into the night.
Yes, we'll be together at the pearly white gate.
We will know only love there won't be any hate.
Our souls will join as one as we enter for all time.
And we will be forever eternally entwined.







WEEP NO MORE

Weep no more for it is I my love,
Back form my world of emotionless hell.
Back into the world of feelings and dreams
I cannot tell you how much I've missed you.
How distant I have been from the world.
Gone to a place that I shared with no one.
Alone on my quest
In search of no man's land
An island by itself
Untouchable by man
No mans land.
Dry to the bones
I wore my shield of protection.
From even you
NO bridges to cross over.
No river to canoe
All by myself without a soul!
Now I'm floating back to you.
Riding on the waves of memories
That comes flooding over me!
Reaching out for your arms
The life jacket you now share with me.
Weep no more my love.
For it is I back from no mans land.
Journeying into our hearts destiny!
Come my love and ride the rising tide with me!






Conceptual Reality

 The understanding of my reality,
Mixed in really with all of yours.
Those who read my words, and those that surrounds me.
Family and friends and those acquaintances
That doesn't fit into any particular category.
What is real and what is make believe?
But isn't the imagined also my reality?
The reality within my mind and soul
And doesn't my reality become part of yours
When penned upon this page?
Dreams an escape from reality
Or are they?
Perhaps they are more real then we admit.
What do we judge ourselves by in the norm of this world?
Perhaps shared realities that most of us understand.
Then what is abnormal? A reality I can't comprehend
Something I've yet to experience?
And if it is real to you than who am I to say it's not.
Fantasy or reality
What lye's there deep within the minds soul?
So real the touch of your hands and the beating of your hearts
They say I live within my own little world.
Yet my world is broader than theirs by far.
I can travel anywhere in my reality.
And I don't even need a car.
They look thru me and think they see who I am.
But it is only their own reality that they seek.
They cannot see into my souls reality unless I let them.
They are blind to my world as they are blind to their own.
They who try to live in others realities.
I choose my reality and I choose to share it with you.
Which makes you part of it.  Yet you deny that you know me.
I have opened the doors and let you in.
To the souls dreams and desires.
Fantasy you say to me, Na it is my reality.
A world of existence where I go from time to time
Living in two spheres of life.
I laugh when I hear you say, ``But that isn't real."
What pray tell then is real?
The air that we breathe,
How simple the answer is to the question you seek.
It is all-real.  As real as real can be.
Therefore don't doubt when your told things you do not understand.
Some people would say that I am crazy.
Or perhaps I hear voices in my mind.
Some would try and medicate me for what I have said.
But mark my words, as they are the truth of things.
Everywhere I see and touch and feel and say and hear and imagine
Is all my reality?
And it is also my sanity.  Imagined or not.
Experienced myself or through others.
It's what I have retained inside.
It guides me through my day.
Come now share through my poetry
My real belief
My faith,
My Conceptual Reality!

MELODY

My brain is ticking but it just isn't me
While sitting here and writing this poetry!
I can hear the rhythm now it's here in my head
It's even with me when I go to bed.
It sounds like the beating of a leather drum
And keeps on pounding pa pum pa pum pa pum!
The rhythm gets louder and it drives me insane.
The words don't matter now it's all in the name.
The beating of the drum turns out to be me.
I can now hear the words with the melody!!







ECSTASY


My heart rate beats rapidly from your lips ravaging mine.
My body quivers with uncontrolled desires touched by you.
Yearning to be ignited with your love like a match to gasoline,
Thoughts turn into eternal love making in your arms.
Held captive in your warm embrace in this moment!
Wanton desires expelling from within my soul.
Joining us into one union of hot body mass.
Our aura reeks of sweat and mixed aftershave and perfume.
We sway to the pounding music that drives us into one and other.
A force driving us both now we have no power over it.
It consumes us into another sphere.  Lifting us higher and higher.
Until we climax with an explosion of fireworks of emotions into
Ecstasy!!







MY SWEET LOVE


Oh my sweet love, how could you misjudge me so?
On playful words exchanged.
Is it not enough to know?
How close my heart beats to your?
How my mind freely engages yours?
Do you not feel the same ache of hunger?
I'm starving inside!
Our destiny awaits us my love.
Wrapped in each other's spirit.
Fear not for I hold your heart within mine.
Guarded with the own beating of my own.
Fell our spirits entwining.
Braiding us into one spirit.
Come my love embark upon this journey.
For it has always been about the journey for us my love.
Yet our destiny awaits us.
The sun is shining its rays of love down upon us.
Fear not what we share my love.
For I am but a human being!  I am not without faults my love.
Is it I you fear my love or you?
I am but a mirror of your reflection.
Oh my sweet, sweet love. How could you misjudge me so?

DOORKEEPER

 
Have you seen the doorkeeper?
Keeper of all time
The doorman that stands
At the gateway to your soul
He lets no one in
Except those you know.
Oh, have you seen the keeper
Of all secrets held within.
I hear somebody knocking
But I'm afraid to let them in.
Oh, have you seen the doorman
That stands guard to my soul.
He will know if you are friend or foe
And keep you in his tow.
He comes round bout eventide
When your emotions begin to hide.
And daydreams turn into night dreams
Engraving feelings upon your soul.
Oh, have you seen the doorkeeper
Keeper of my soul
Oh, there he is in your doorway
Making a hallway from me to you
Letting secrets through
Allowing you to see into me
And me into you!
Keeper of all time
He stands guard down home
Round bout eventide.
When our emotions begin to rise
Not letting anyone inside
Except perhaps you!






SET ME FREE
 
This emptiness inside
Like a dark hollow in a tree
Haven to birds that make spring nest.
Void now of all emotions

I sit and watch as birds feed
Their young, tiny beaks grabbing
On worms from their mother
I marvel at their new life and I wonder.

Yet still void I sit here in myself
Created hole.
Darkness surrounds me as I
Search for life within

Oh, creator of creatures both small and big
Have I nothing left to give?
As the wind gently blows,
Breathing life

Into my soul once again
That I may feel some emotions
Even pain is welcome today.
I just want the numbness to go away.

As I approach another day.
Help me appreciate what I've thrown away.
That I might toss into the wind
This emptiness that fills me once again

And feel the warmth of the sun once more
Your gentle tap upon my door
Your tender caress that last all through the night.
Help me appreciate the little things.

A blooming rose sending scents of perfume into the air.
Children swinging and laughing with glee
Birds chirping way up in a tree
The sun now warm upon my back.

As emptiness fades into the starry night
I have once again found my guiding light.
As I wish I may I wish I might?
Upon the first star I see tonight.

Thanking GOD that I can write.
Burring the dead inside of me.
Once again I am set free
I find myself in poetry.

I am the one you let into your mind and soul
I am the one that you don't know
Have we met?
I've traveled this road many times before
It seems our paths have crossed before this day!
A blind friend to tell your woes
Another soul on a journey of a poet's life!
Who travels deep within the recesses of your mind!
 I am the one that hears your crying.
 I am the one that takes the time.
To read your poetry
And see your dreams and visions of life.
 I AM THE ONE!
It is not about the destination of the journey we are all on.
It is after all and has always been about life's journey!

 




A DIME

I write the pretty words of poetry.
That speaks of love and smiling faces.
You think when you read me I am alive.
But I live upon this paper.
And this vast Internet.  Yes, I am this person you read.
I do feel, dream, love and hurt.
But take away the fancy words and all the rhyme.
And I am just a dime you found on the ground.
You picked me up and put me in your pocket.
And you'll spend me with the rest of your change.
But you won't even wonder from where I came.
The thought wont cross your mind whose pocket I've been in beflre.
You won't know that I was once a dollar.
That someone made into change.
You won't even know I have a name.
You wont know that once I was a quarter.
That someone else found.
That I lay there along time before you came around.
You won't even wonder about the dirt upon my face.
You wont think about how it felt to be left there on the ground.
In the cold of the night all-alone in the dark.
You will just know that you have a dime you found along the way.
And you'll go shopping and spend me.
And I will once again be on my journey.
Perhaps I'll be found again.
When someone rummages thru these pages.
Or the next time you take a dime from your pocket.
Just perhaps you'll think of me.
And you'll stop to ponder on my words this day.
And wonder why you feel you now know
What it's like to be a dime on the ground.
Found!






CAN YOU FEEL ME BABY

I'm reaching out to touch you.
Can you feel my hand upon your face?
Gently feeling the softness of your flesh.
Your smiling at me now and looking deep into my dreamy blue eyes.
I never knew what love looked like until I saw it there.
It was expounding from the deep recesses of your beating heart.
And I could see the desire and the hot flame within you.
It makes me feel warm and content inside.
It tugs at my heart and soul more than you know.
It makes me melt in your arms and give you all of me.
It allows me to trust you and open up.
I tell you all the deep secrets in my soul.
Holding nothing back I give it all.
I let my guards down when you enter me.
And I feel the fullness of you inside.
It is like the hunger of a starving child.
Craving for the comfort of your embrace.
Going higher and higher into the ecstasy of our lovemaking
Pulling you closer and closer within me.
I now smell the aura of you all over my trembling body.
I breath deeply and take you all in.
I too look into your eyes and show you the love
there within my beating heart.
My touch soft and sensual responding now to yours
Our souls join as one hot flame of desire.
And we become one body mass of passions delight.
Rocking slowly now locked within each other's arms.
We enjoy the feel of each other's bodies.
I can hear the music of our love making as we rock to the rhythm of
The music within our souls
Heated now with the flame of our desires we explode!
Laying here now enjoying the release of tension
Feeling only the content ness of one and other.
I'm reaching out to you!

Can you feel me baby?






HIDE AND SEEK


I hid within my silent world
Away from all to see
Even from those I love
Living in my self-made fantasy.

I hid in dark places
Even in the recesses of my mind.
I hid from soft memories
When at once I thought you was mine.

I hid behind my mortar wall.
Where you couldn't see.
Each brick that lay there
Was a tiny part of me!

I heard no songs played or melody.
Nor did I rock to any lullabies.
Instead I choose to hide.
Where no one heard my cries.

With hidden fear and soul
I closed my door
To life and it's laughter
And happily ever after,

Hidden in my silent world
As the clock ticked on,
All by myself there
As the days grew long!

But I hid from my reality
From what I held deep inside of me
I felt the need to embrace you once again.
To know you as my friend,

So now I come out
From playing hide and seek.
As I can hear the call
All Lee All in Free!

So please do forgive me
For hiding for so long,
But this time for introspection,
Has taught me a very good lesson.

That life is what I make it.
And it's self-contained,
And love is to be embraced.
Without fear or shame.

That it doesn't really matter
What I write along the way,
Some good, some bad,
As long as I write it every day!

After all, all of life is poetry
In everything we do.
And it's up to you and me
To live it thru and thru!!

"LOVE'S SILENT DESCENT"

She walked on to the edge of the water.
Silent in her descent
Eyes cast down looking at the nothingness
That filled the brisk mornings air.
How long had she walked?
Hours in the dark of night!
Alone wondering around until the morning light.
Searching she was for the answers
That lye so deep within her soul
Exhausted now from the long cold walk,
She collapsed upon the ground
Feeling weary inside
Like something had died!
Was this the end then?
Time to give up on hope itself?
She lay there and wept a thousand tears.
Gasping for air
She looked up one last time
Into the morning sunrise
There he was
A ray of hope
Painted across the sky's canvas.
With his bronze wet body
And clinging black hair,
Dripping from a mornings dip.
He was as a sun god.
Golden orange in the morning's tangerine rays!
Walking towards her ever so sure in his stride.
She could only stare as he approached her
Within arms reach he slowly spoke to her.
"I've missed you!"
Embracing
Silent in their descent!

LISTEN UNTO ME

Listen unto me now, all that can hear.
Abide in my words this day
Bend and kneel to pray.
I will return one day,
In all the glory of this name,
The world will never be the same!

I am the Father, I proclaim,
In all the heaven's and the earth.
I abide in you that believe.
Look into your hearts that need.
Fulfill my prophecy one and all.
I tell you now great
Nations will fall!!

Many innocent souls will perish
In this battle in the name of goodness
This is as I have said,
The beginning of times to come
Comfort one onto another.
For I will tell you
I will come when
This world knows great pain.
And I will pluck you from it
In my Holy name!

Be ye comforted to know.
That what I speak is true.
Journey back into the light!
All that are in the light will not perish.
Be not blinded by the darkness
Within the world

I hear my children cry.
Turn not your hearts cold
Least I come not for you.
Let the warmth of my light
Warm you heart and soul.
Find a place within to hide.
Stay clear of all that do evil,
In the name of goodness!

I am a GOD of love
All that know this love
I have chosen to be at my side.
Love one another.
Judge not what your brother does
For you know not his heart.

I will remain in you and you in me.
Seek me out spiritually and there
Ye will find me.
Listen carefully for my words these days.
And know that I am with you always.
Have I not said I will not forsake you?
Seek and you shall find
Right within your heart,
Spread ye my words
So that others might also hear.

And be ready,
The time grows near…

``SADNESS IN HER SMILE"

That I trace within my mind.
A calmness there such beauty that
Endures the harsh realities of her world.
Lost she is within her own soul.
Searching for her voice.
Weeping at life as it passes by.
Strangers all of them, that stop
To gaze her way and yet
She begs them to stay.
To feel something inside
Perhaps to give a sigh,
Cold she feels as the night air,
Cold inside the depth of her soul,
Searching to touch her inner being.
Afraid of someone seeing
Her for the first time,
She must live out her life
As she writes it daily
Drama of a human being
Touched by an angel once.
And now she hears his voice.
Sadness there that she was left behind.
Here on earth to do his will.
As she struggles to be heard
And taken seriously.

Than the one she carries
as deep as the stormy blue sea.
A poet of the soul truly!!!
She feels what she writes
And writes what she feels.
And is transformed into a mood
That carries her like the wind.
Blowing, blowing once again.
But there lies the sadness in her smile
That I trace within my mind…

Relentless

He said you are relentless
And I had to smile as
I thought about what that
Word meant to me.
I even took the time to look it
Up in the dictionary.
Just to be sure I understood
What he meant when he said it.
And even when I read it
I knew it was I.
Relentless was a reality.
For I'm driven from within
By forces I don't know.
But it's there in my poetry.
And when I'm alone I can see.
How relentless I believe
You see if you're true to your heart
You can't help but be
Relentless to the core
It burrows down into your soul.
And takes you where you want to go.
