NET STOP

Description | Syntax | Parameters | Switches | Related | Notes | Examples | Errorlevels | Availability

Stopping a service cancels any network connections the service is using. Also, some services are dependent on others. Stopping one service can stop others.

Services cannot be stopped from a command prompt within Windows.

Syntax

NET HELP STOP

NET STOP [BASIC | NWREDIR | WORKSTATION | NETBEUI | NWLINK] [/YES]

NET STOP service

To stop the workgroup redirector, type:

NET STOP

(This breaks all your connections to shared resources and removes the NET commands from your computer's memory).

NETBEUI (v7.00)

Switches

Related

Notes

The Eventlog service cannot be stopped.
