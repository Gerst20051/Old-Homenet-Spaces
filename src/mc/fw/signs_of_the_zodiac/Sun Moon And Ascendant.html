Sun Moon And Ascendant

SUN  
The Sun represents the center from which your life energy flows, the creative energy that fuels your sense of purpose. Your Sun radiates out and illuminates all your other planets with light and energy.

If you fail to recognize and express your true self, then you may lose the balance between ego and the larger self. You may lose all enthusiasm; feeling stuck and without purpose; or stubborn pride and arrogance can move outward and distort all your other planetary energies.

The sign of your Sun describes the kind of creative energy you use to focus and center all your vital energies. The House position of your Sun describes the area of life experiences in which you shine with this focused energy, where you express your vitality, where you seek recognition and a sense of purpose.

MOON  
The moon represents the magical, emotional part of you that tells you how to feel contented, how to meet your deepest needs. Moon understands the way of waiting, of learning how to fit in and become a part of the whole; for Moon knows that everything is somehow connect. Moon is one of the indicators of psychic talents.

If you fail to recognize and express the deep emotional part of yourself, you may feel moody and react with childish petulance or fear to the events around you. The Sign of your Moon describes the kind of process you go through in order to feel secure, contented, and fulfilled.

Moon's Sign describes your initial "gut" reaction to sudden and stressful events around you. The House position describes the types of life situations that nourish you, give you inner support, and from which you give nourishment to others. Here is where you seek emotional security.

ASCENDANT
The Ascendant is the Sign that was coming up over the eastern horizon at the instant of your birth. It represents how you present yourself to the world, your own personal style, your unique way of expressing your self in action.

if you fail to recognize and express your own personal style, then you may lack confidence and appear false and unreal to the people around you. The Sign on your Ascendant describes the manner in which you present yourself to the world around you; the energies that you use to "walk in grace", to feel self-confident and centered.
