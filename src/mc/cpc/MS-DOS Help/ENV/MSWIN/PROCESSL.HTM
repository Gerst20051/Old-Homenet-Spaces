%PROCESSOR_LEVEL%

Description | Syntax | Related | Notes | Examples | Availability

Level of the processor (CPU) being used by the machine.

Syntax

%PROCESSOR_LEVEL%

Related

%PROCESSOR_ARCHITECTURE%
%PROCESSOR_IDENTIFIER%
%PROCESSOR_REVISION%

Notes
  • Family 4 Intel 486, AMD 5x86, Cyrix 5x86
  • Family 5 Intel Pentium and Pentium MMX, AMD K5 and K6, Cyrix 6x86, all Centaur/IDT WinChip, Rise mP6, Transmeta
  • Family 6 Intel Pentium Pro, Celeron, Pentium II and Pentium III, AMD Athlon, Cyrix 6x86MX and M-II
  • Family 7 Intel Itanium (IA-64)
Examples 
    PROCESSOR_LEVEL=5
Availability
Static

none
