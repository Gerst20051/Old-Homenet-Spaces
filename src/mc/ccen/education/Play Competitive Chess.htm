Play Competitive Chess

Course Description:

Chess is a game of competition and strategy. But most of all it is fun. The basic moves and rules can be learned in an hour, yet it can take a lifetime to master. Chess requires focus and thinking, planning, and evaluating outcomes. Do you have desire to learn and improve your game? This interest course will provide instruction and practice aimed at making your chess game more competitive. Those choosing this course should have the basic rules of the game, but beginner to experienced players are welcome. We have chess sets available for 20 players. Its your move.
