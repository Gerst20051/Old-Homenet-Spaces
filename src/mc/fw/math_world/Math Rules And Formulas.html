Commonly Used Formulas

Density

D = mass/volume

Percent Error

%error = |actual - observed| x 100 actual

Percent Yield

%yield = actual yield x 100 theoretical yield

Specific Heat

Q = (m)(DT)(s)
Q = heat lost or gained
m = mass of substance
DT = final temp - initial temp
s = specific heat of substance

Kelvin-Celsius Conversion

K = oC + 273

Molarity

M = moles solute
liters solution

Boyle's Law

P1V1 = P2V2
(T and n constant)

Charles' Law

V1 / T1 = V2 / T2
(T in Kelvin; P and n constant)

Gay-Lussac's Law

P1 / T1 = P2 / T2
(T in Kelvin; V and n constant)

Combined Gas Law

P1 V1 / T1 = P2 V2 / T2
(T in Kelvin; n constant)

Ideal Gas Law

PV = nRT
(R = ideal gas constant)

Dalton's Law of Partial Pressures

Pt = P1 + P2 +...+ Pn

wavelength-frequency relationship

c = ln
c = speed of light (3.00 x 108 m/sec)
l = wavelength
n = frequency

Planck's Law

E = hn
E = energy of one quantum
h = Planck's Constant (6.63 x 10-34 J sec)
n = frequency
