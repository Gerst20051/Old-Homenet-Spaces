Quiet, Advanced and Versatile

The Beltronics Pro RX65 is the most advanced radar, laser and safety detector ever designed by Beltronics.

The Beltronics Pro RX65 includes full X, K, SuperWide Ka, and Safety Warning System radar capability, front and rear laser detection, digital signal processing (DSP) for superior range and reduced false alarms, our patented Mute and AutoMute, audible and visual band alerts, and all the performance you'd expect from Beltronics.

Four selectivity functions virtually eliminate false alerts, and Threat Display™, a sophisticated means of tracking multiple radar signals, coupled with Digital Voice Technology, keep you informed like no other detector on the market.

Of course, you can trust BEL's proven ability to outperform where it counts most - industry leading sensitivity to today's low-powered traffic radar guns.
