Golf For Beginners

Course Description:

Have you ever wondered what par, birdie, and boogie or pin means in golf? Have you ever wanted to learn how to hit a golf ball? Have you ever wanted to know basic golf techniques and/or terminology? you answered, "yes" to any or all of these questions, then this is the Interest Group for you. We will learn how to correctly grip a golf club, hit a golf ball, and keep score all while learning golf etiquette.
