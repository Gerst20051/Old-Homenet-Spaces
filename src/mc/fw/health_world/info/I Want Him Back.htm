So You Want Him Back, eh?

Well, are you really really sure? At this point I should warn you that if HE dumped YOU then most likely he won't want you back and you should head straight for the 'Heal Ur Broken Heart' section of the website at once.

If however you dumped him and then realized you loved him, or whatever, read on. Try whichever of the following you like to get him back:

1) GROVEL

If you dumped him then chances are he'll be feeling pretty heartbroken himself. If you're lucky he'll jump straight back into your arms, if not, you've got some SERIOUS groveling to do, girlfriend! Apologies from the bottom of your heart. Tell him you now realize what a big mistake you made.

2) REMINISCE

Once you're on speaking terms again, reminisce on all the good times you had together. Talk to him about it. Bring up all your special places in conversation with him. The instant you get him to admit, 'Yeah, we had fun,' come back with 'Wouldn't it be great if we had another chance?'

3) MAKE HIM JEALOUS

Yes, spite breeds spite and I wouldn't recommend it. But this is a good one to used if HE dumped YOU (and if you DIDN'T take my advice and go to the Heal Ur Broken Heart section). Don't go out with some poor boy you don't really like just to make your ex-boy jealous---that's horrible. Instead, make him THINK you have a whole crowd of waiting, jostling boys. Flirt with his friends. It might work, it does on a lot of boys, but I really wouldn't recommend it. It's spiteful.
