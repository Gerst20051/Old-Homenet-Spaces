MicroSoft Operating System/2 (MS OS/2) 1.0

Notes | Added | Changed | Removed | Files
  1. Released 1987.
  2. Designed and developed specifically to harness the capabilities of personal computers based upon the Intel 80286 and 80386 microprocessors.
  3. This is the first product to be announced as a result of the Joint Development Agreement between IBM and Microsoft announced in August 1985.
  4. Intended Replacement for MS-DOS.
  5. Written in assembly.
