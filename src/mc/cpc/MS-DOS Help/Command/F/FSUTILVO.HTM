FSUTIL VOLUME

Description | Syntax | Parameters | Switches | Related | Notes | Examples | Errorlevels | Availability

Manages a volume. Dismounts a volume or queries to see how much free space is available on a disk.

Syntax

FSUTIL VOLUME help

FSUTIL VOLUME diskfree drive:

FSUTIL VOLUME dismount drive

Parameters
diskfree (NTXP)
Queries the free space of a volume.
dismount (NTXP)
Dismounts a volume.
drive (NTXP)
Specifies the drive letter (followed by a colon), mount point, or volume name.
drive: (NTXP)
Specifies the drive letter (followed by a colon).
(NT2003)
Specifies the drive letter (followed by a colon), mount point, or volume name.
help (NTXP)
Display help.
Switches

none.
