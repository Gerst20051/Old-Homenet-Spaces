Names for Irish Pets

Many people have sent me e-mails asking for good names for their pets. Whether you have an Irish Wolfhound, an Irish Setter, a Kerry Blue Terrier or a mutt or cat needing an Irish name, here are some that we feel are appropriate. You can pick a name based on your pet's color, or by personality traits, other physical characteristics and other miscellaneous traits. There is also a section devoted to names for dogs. Perhaps you like names like 'precious' and 'sweetie' for your pets. Here is a list of terms of endearment you might like to use.

Names Based on Color

One of the most common ways of naming a pet is to find a name based on their color. We all know cats named Butterscotch and dogs named Blackie. These color names are divided into lists meaning dark-colored, black, or brown, White, light-colored or yellow/blonde , reddish ,and blue and grey.

Names Meaning Dark or Black.

Usually, when a name means "black", it's referring to hair color. Darcy, means, literally 'descendant of the dark one'. The implied meaning is actually 'descendant of the dark-haired one', not that Darcy's ancestor was some kind of evil, dark character.

Branduff BRAN duf raven-black brandubh

Names Meaning Blue and Grey

Blue is actually a pretty popular name for dogs. While I have never seen a dog that was actually blue, there are many hues of black and grey which are considered to be blue. Perhaps you'd also want to give this color name to a dog or cat who is your true blue friend, or a regal blue-blooded purebred.
