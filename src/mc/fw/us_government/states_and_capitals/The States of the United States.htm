The States of the United States

The following is a list of all states in the United States. Click on any of the headings to sort by that value.

  • "Date" is the date of admission into the Union, or the date of ratification of the Constitution for the first 13 states.
  • "Gov Term" indicates the term, in years, of the state's Governor
  • "Leg" indicates the type of legislature the state has - "Bi" for bicameral, "Uni" for unicameral
  • "Leg Term" indicates the term, in years, of the state's lower and upper house (or the single house if unicameral)
  • "EV" indicates the number of electoral votes the state has in Presidential elections, as of the most recent census
  • "Lower" indicates the number of members of the state's lower house
  • "Upper" indicates the number of members of the state's upper house
  • "Population" is as of the 2000 census
