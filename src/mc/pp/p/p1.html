MY LITTLE COTTAGE HOME

Welcome to my country cottage home
Step onto the stepping-stones
Within my life and gaze upon my flower beds
Full of day lilies bowing their orange and yellow heads
Meander with me round the lilac bushes
Where scents of lavender float thru the air
The old tree stump still lives. Trimmed out as a hedge now
Holding a private birds bath nicely there.
And over yonder another towering tree stump.
No life left in it except the wedge wood painted
Birdhouse towering atop its seven-foot trunk
Now sanctuary to a family of sparrows,
Stopping to ponder upon the flowerbeds
Where resting lilies now raise their heads.
Bright pink coneflowers, bee balm and butterfly weeds
Where an orange butterfly stops to rest its weary wings.
Passing by the arbor you will find the harbor
For freshly planted clematis Just starting to climb up on the lattice
Up the stairs we go now. To our porch like deck
Green treated and sealed with water sealant to protect its wood from rotting.
Thirty some pots sit all about.  It's a glorious sight without a doubt.
Like walking into a green house in full bloom.
Only you're really in my outdoor floral room.
Here rainbows of moss rose grow in blue flowered metal teapots.
Hot pink impatiens and white double-headed begonias thrive in old
Plastic containers with spikes and climbing ivy trailing over the tops
And purple pansies in a rustic old gray metal basket.
A gliding bench for two lovers to share And a black cast iron table with chairs.
The finch feeder hanging in the tree with yellow finches are eating.
But I always call them yellow canaries.
Bird haven are my two-maple trees
With a coconut house and acorn one too along with
That old bird feeder they made into a nest.
What fun watching them gather grass to build a place for them to rest.
You'll hear the sweet melodies of the birds singing and chirping their songs.
And the water falling from my old whisky barrel patio pond
And watch the gold fish swim around.
I could stay here forever. Where I know I belong.
Harbored in this flowery sanctuary.
Where I stroll around each day along my stepping stone path in life.






HIS LOVE IS LIMITLESS

It saddens me when I think of you
I feel as blue as God's limitless skyway
And I don't know what to do.
I can sense your pain this day.
And if I could I would take it all away!

So many unspoken dreams!
One of God's angels has fallen
And he is hurting.
Head bowed between my knees
I will pray for you.
With tears falling where they please.
I hope you'll pray too.
Some time's it's all we can do

God is loving and kind
He helps us to find
Our true inner strength
Look deep within your heart
Allow him to embrace your soul again.
Letting your pain and sorrow
Spill over into the wasteland of tomorrow.

But for today
Allow him to erase it all and take it away.
Tis what I pray for you.
Not knowing what else I could do.
I pray for happy memories to flood your heart
Soul and mind
Magnified to an intensity
To erase your pain today,
Watching the clouds part
Opening up heavens doorway.

Let God fill you with his majesty
Picking you up off the ground
And healing you inside once again
Where you find yourself deep within.
His stairway up to heaven where
His love is limitless!!






SOMEWHERE IN A PLACE CALLED HEAVEN

Where are you my love?
I've looked above
in the sticky marshmallow clouds
Playing hide and seek within each unique formation.
But where are you my love?
Somewhere in a place called heaven
Is where I search for you my love
Far above the northern stars
Beyond the top of Judy Garland's rainbow
There in the infinity of the vast open skyways.
I see your image forming now.
As the clouds part midway
I can see your peaceful face.
Smiling down at me.
I feel your majestic aura
Carried in the wind.
Your voice plays soft melodies
Of Dreams-Dreams- Dreams
Floating in a puff of a magic cloud.
You come to me once again.
Your presence so strong I can feel
The beat of your heart inside of mine
I feel your spiritual energy
Climb into my soul and warm me.
Your illusiveness teases me.
So close within my grasp and yet
You drift away once more
On your magic billowing pillow
I will search for you my love
Across this universe and into infinity
Far into the limitless heavens
Until we are once again joined into one formation
Where we float among the stars together
Just you and I.
Somewhere in a place they call heaven!

MOON LOVERS DANCE

Come closer to me now my love.
Guide me into a world full of enchantment
Where gypsies dance their love dance by a glowing campfire.
Vibrant and bold!
We shall dance like the gypsies to the moon dance.
As we become soul mates within each other's warm embrace.
Sealing our love with a trance as we
Gaze within each other's dreamy eyes.
The dancing orange flames memorize us.
Here all secrets are revealed.
Hiding nothing, we enter into one unity.
Free of past discretions and mistakes.
The mysterious full moon shines above
Pouring over us with love
Made pure once again as a sweet scented virgin moonflower
Awakening on this magical night.
We dance for the delight of our love we've found.
Into the cool night breeze
Round and round the campfire.
We toss our heads and snap our heels.
And twirl each other with ease.
Guiding us into a dreamland
Where all our dreams come alive
As we become memorized.
Within the dance of the moonlight
Our souls join binding us
Within the melody of the soft swaying music
Gazing dreamily into each other's eyes.
Leaving us to dance the moon lover's dance
Into the destiny of our eternal light!

OVER THE LINE

Hello my sweet, sweet, love
Has it not been forever since we have spoken?
I can see your tender lips
Up on your dark handsome face
Making me sense the anticipation of our first kiss.
As I gasp for a breath of air.
Moist, soft, sensual and lingering
Like an all day sucker, sweet and tangy
The welled up yearning passion from deep within
Driving me insane once again.
Imagined here in my mind.
Can it be a glimmer of feelings?
Tucked away inside of my soul.
My heart melting away from the winter cold
Like snow melts in morning's sunlight?
Dare I to step over the line?
To entertain the thought that you are mine,
To trust you not to break my heart in two,
Like a broken wishbone and a broken dream
Just to be myself with you?
Dare I my sweet, sweet love
Become something else to you in time?
To make memories last of yours and mine
Photographs within our mind
Our hearts entwined
Like a braided rope lassoing our hearts.
Oh but I would welcome the knots you tie
Deep with in my belly
If just for a moment my lips
Might brush ever so slightly up against yours
If you were more tangible than a dream
I would make you scream
In loving ecstasy!
Dare I step over the line of my imagination?
My sweet, sweet love!






REAL

I opened my door up once again
Allowed my self-feelings to seep within
My lonely heart Broken in time
Like the old coo coo clock of my mothers
Discarded now in an old box
Still waiting to be fixed.

I thought it would be a new start
Of what I didn't know
I just wanted things to flow
Like a river running up hill
But no matter where I go
Past events haunt me.
These feelings a glimpse of my reality

It's hard for me to feel today
Now I want those same feelings to just go away.
I could feel that closing door once again.
It was barely open like it'd never been.
I tried to stick my foot into it.
But I found it no longer fit.
Oh if I could just bottle it all up.
And keep it frozen like an icebox.
I could go through life
Void of any real emotion
Just going through the motions.
But then I'd miss that smile of yours.
And I would no longer have that glimmer of hope.
That one day I might be just like you!!
Real!!!

THESE FEELINGS


These feelings are like wounds cut deep within my skin
Bleeding and healing over and over again
They are like the rainbow colors after the storm.
The glowing warmth of sunshine when I'm forlorn
They are like a roller coaster going up and down.
Like a Ferris wheel that can't reach the ground
They are like the air that we breathe
Invisible and untouchable to an extent
They are like yarn all tangled up and worn.
They are like a child climbing a tree.
Or a baby kitten looking for its mommy
They are how we think and more what we perceive.
They are a part of you and a part of me.
They are what I clothe to disguise them from you.
So you won't know weather I'm happy or blue.
They are external surely not inside of me.
I can just pretend it's all in a movie.
Just play the act upon the stage
Never knowing the end
Oh, but here come those feelings seeping in.

SHE SLEEPS

Staring out my patio door
Into my back yard
That extends into an open bean field
I watch as all my lovely flowers bow their head
Turning brown like a paper bag from season's change.

The grasshopper's racked havoc
Amongst them this year with their green long legs
And small trails of dead barren grass
Can be seen where the neighborhood mole
Has tunneled his way to China.

Already I am anticipating
The work to be done next spring
Trimming back the dead
Once colorful blooms like getting a haircut.
Soon to rake the falling leafs around

Their roots to provide winter's warmth.
I gaze upwards at the now changing leafs
Of my maple tree's Brown and yellow
With shades of orange beginning to paint
The tree like on a canvas

Picture of nature's beauty.
I feel the closure here as I gaze
Upon the vast openness that prepares herself to sleep
My potted plants half awake now.
With sleepy blooms

Of colors like in a rainbow
Splattered amongst them.
Taking every blink they can to stay awake.
It has been a long hot summer.
And I am ready for the change.

Changing of seasons and events.
Like waking up fresh in the morning
And adorning a new set of clothes
There is new life here in the passing of time
I nod my head at nature's process

Of continual life I marvel at her
Way of returning into the blackened earth.
Her place of rebirth.  Sleep my beauty sleep.
Rest within the loving folds of Mother Nature!

HAPPY DAUGHTERS DAY


I spent the day with my mum today
We had such fun working together under the sun.
We visited the local nursery
And picked out pastel colored pansies,
Deep red geraniums, yellow dahlias and mixed moss rose.
Tall spikes and dusty miller to put into pots
And wavy purple petunias with green potato vines that grow and grow.
We hauled them all home and began to plant.
Using our artistic talents to arrange them all just so.  In the old gray washtubs they Went and some into the flower beds in the front ditch by the road.
Oh where does the time go? The day is almost through.
What a lovely day we had just us two.
Bonding together mother and daughter under the sun.
Admiring our work once it was done.
And with Mother's day past I thought it only fit.
To declare this day Daughter's Day as I spent it with mom
But then every day we find time to spend together is one.

After all if it weren't for mothers a daughter wouldn't be.
Happy Mother's Day mom
And happy Daughter's Day to me!!







MY REALM OF SERENITY

I awaken to sloppy dog kisses
And the sun shining bright through my window
As I stumble out of my bed.  I hurry to the bathroom
Then slip on my shoes to take the dogs outside.
The morning air is cool and brisk.

I look around me in awe at all the lovely flowers on my garden deck
Bowing their heads as if praying for a morning shower
And I am blessed with all the colors of the rainbow glistening before me.
I play Frisbee with my black lab Bear

And watch him fly through the air like an acrobat catching it.
As I stroll around to my back yard following
my terra cotta stepping stone path
I laugh at Popeye my chiwawa as he steals the Frisbee
And runs away as fast as his legs will carry him.
Round and round the lilac bushes he goes.

I stop for a few moments to gaze upon
Each one of my flower beds looking for new buds of life.
Smelling the sweet lavender scent of lilacs.
I reminisce on all the labor of love
That has gone into my humble home
Taking a deep breath of air
I realize I am content in my realm of serenity.

My home!
