Planets In Aspect
Back

Any Planets brought into connection by any aspect affect each other according to their nature and the type of aspect. Major aspects to your Sun, Moon, & Ascendant have the most powerful impact on your life. After you become familiar with energy of the major aspects, you may wish to explore one of the many excellent texts on astrology.

The value of the aspect is the number of degrees that defines the aspect, and the orb of influence is the number of degrees on either side of exact that is considered still influential. (may vary with different astrologers)

Transits

Only the Major aspects are considered when comparing the planets in the current sky to your natal planets [position of the planets at your exact time of birth] The orb [of influence] allowed for transits is usually only 1 degree.
